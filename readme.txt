== Description ==

Online Payments is a simple and secure WordPress plugin, enabling your clients to make payments with a click of a button. 

= Key features of Online Payments plugin: =

*	Process unlimited amounts every month
* 	Add as many payment buttons to your site pages & posts 
* 	Customize the payment button to perfectly match your website theme and 	design
* 	Accept payments using your preferred payment gateway - Paypal, Stripe or Square
*	Set your preferred currency from 26 supported currencies  
* 	Enable fast checkout to your clients with vcita's secured card on file 	feature
* 	Automatically send thank you email with every successful payment 
* 	Get notified automatically with every successful payment 
* 	Get notified of late payment and send reminders to clients
*	Issue invoices and email them to clients with an invitation to pay 
	online  
* 	Integrate vCita with QuickBooks or other accounting & billing services 	via Zapier
* 	Specify a price and description for your product or let your client manually fill on checkout 
* 	Ask the client to add any type of data you want on checkout 	
*      Charge clients' credit cards directly from your dashboard
* 	Keep track of all your billing activity & payments. You can also export it to CSV file

FREE 14 DAYS TRIAL. No credit card needed


== Installation ==
 
= Install Online Payments =
1.	Download “WP PayPal and Stripe Online Payments By vCita” and activate it.
2.	Connect your WordPress site with you vcita account. You may connect an existing account or create an account via the plugin by simply providing your Email address. This Email will be used for notifications from vcita only.
3.	Connect a payment gateway - either PayPal or Stripe, and Select your payment currency.
4.	Customize your payment button style, label and amount or title, and add it to any page or post using shortcode.
5. 	To double the number of leads generated on your site, add the vcita LiveSite widget. Simply click on the LiveSite widget tab on the right and make sure it's turned on.

= Manage all payment on your Back office =
The vcita back office allows you to easily manager all your customers and payments. To access your payments summary:
1.	Click on "Back office" on the right navigation bar
2. 	Navigate to "Payments"
